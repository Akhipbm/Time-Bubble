Privacy Policy for Time Bubble

Time Bubble does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information from users. All functionality — including analog/digital display, timezone selection, and 12H/24H switching — runs entirely on your device using local code contained within the extension package.

No browsing data, cookies, history, passwords, personal information, usage analytics, or identifiable data is accessed or collected. The extension does not send any data to external servers or third-party services.

Time Bubble operates fully offline and does not require or use an internet connection.
